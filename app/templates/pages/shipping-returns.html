Shipping & Returns

Last modified: Feb 01 2021

When your order will ship

In most cases, your order will ship within 1-2 business days. We'll send you an email when it ships along with a confirmation email that has your delivery service information and tracking number. If part of your order isn't available for immediate shipment, your order will not ship until all items are available. You will see “Processing” if you check the status of your order online. When your order ships, the status will change to “Completed”, and you’ll receive a shipment notice with a tracking number.

Shipping methods and carriers that we use

In the US, we offer FedEx Next Day, Second Day, and Ground shipping to physical addresses. Shipping is not available to PO boxes or military bases. In Canada, we offer UPS Express and UPS Ground shipping to physical addresses (no PO Boxes).

International shipping

All orders placed on the Nest store must have the same country for the shipping and billing address. If you want to make a purchase in another country, please visit a local Nest retailer or place an order with them online. You can see where Nest products are available on our Where to buy page.
